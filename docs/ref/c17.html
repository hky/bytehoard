Chapter 1. Introduction

This document is a reference about anything relevant in the ByteHoard code. It's designed to save you hours of trawling through the code to see what stuff does and what parameters to give things, and will hopefully make whatever you're trying to do easier.

It is nowhere near finished - bear in mind this is a one-man job thing, so it may take a while. But remember - good things might come to those who wait.
