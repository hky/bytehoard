Chapter 2. Basic Usage

2.1. Logging In

First you need to open the Login screen by clicking the 'Login' link from the main page. You'll be asked for your username and password; type these in the textboxes and then click 'Login'. You might have to wait a few seconds, but you should then be logged in and presented with the main ByteHoard interface.

If your login doesn't succeed, then remember that passwords are case-sensitive; make sure that any capitalisation is the same as before and that your Caps Lock and your Num Lock (if you are on a laptop or using a compact keyboard) are both off. If you still can't login, you might have to ask for your password to be reset.
